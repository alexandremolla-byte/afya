FAQ

Common questions

Is AFYA a replacement for my doctor? ▾
No. AFYA is a health companion, not a medical provider. It helps you track your health and understand your condition better — but it cannot diagnose, prescribe, or replace clinical care. Always consult your doctor for diagnosis and treatment.
Do I need to download an app? ▾
No download needed. AFYA is a web app — open it in any browser at getafya.co/app.html. On Android Chrome, you can tap "Add to Home Screen" for a native app feel with its own icon.
How does the referral system work? ▾
Every AFYA user gets a personal referral code. Share it with a friend — when they sign up and enter your code during onboarding, you both instantly get 1 month of Premium free, with no payment required. Your free months stack, so the more you refer, the longer your Premium lasts.
Is my health data safe? ▾
Your data is stored in an encrypted database (Supabase). We never sell your health data or share it with advertisers. Your AI conversations are processed securely and are not used to train AI models.
What payment methods does AFYA accept? ▾
AFYA uses Paystack for payments. You can pay with any Nigerian bank card (Verve, Mastercard, Visa), bank transfer, or USSD. All payments are processed securely by Paystack.
